# -WaveCon-Telecom-5G-Network-Impact-Analysis
### Business Impact Analysis of 5G Launch | Power BI + DAX + Power Query

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **business impact of WaveCon Telecom's 5G network launch** — completed as part of the **#CodeBasics Virtual Internship** at **AtliQ Technologies**.

The objective was to evaluate how the 5G rollout influenced **revenue, customer behavior, plan performance, churn, and network KPIs** — and translate those findings into **clear, actionable business recommendations.**

> 💡 *"Not just what happened after 5G — but WHY it happened, and what to do next."*

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

> Evaluate how the 5G rollout influenced:
> - 📊 Revenue trends (before vs after)
> - 👥 Customer adoption & churn behavior
> - 📋 Plan performance (winning vs obsolete)
> - 🌐 Network KPIs (speed, latency, uptime)
> - 🏙️ City-level growth and decline markets

---

## 🔑 Key Metrics & Highlights

| Metric | Finding |
|---|---|
| 📉 Churn Reduction (5G users) | **~23% lower** than 4G users |
| 🏆 Top Performing Plan | **P1 (Premium)** — consistent outperformer |
| 📉 Declining Plans | **P7–P9 (Legacy)** — sharp decline post 5G |
| 🏙️ Growth Markets | Lucknow, Patna, Raipur — positive revenue growth |
| ⚠️ Pressure Markets | Delhi, Chennai — revenue pressure post 5G |
| 🚀 Network Speed | 500+ Mbps supporting premium adoption |

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Analysis

### 📊 Before vs After 5G — Revenue Impact

| Market Type | Cities | Trend |
|---|---|---|
| 📈 Growth Markets | Lucknow, Patna, Raipur | Positive revenue growth post 5G |
| ⚠️ Pressure Markets | Delhi, Chennai | Revenue pressure — competition + plan cannibalization |

> **Insight:** Tier-2 cities benefited most from 5G launch while metro markets faced competitive pressure — suggesting a **Tier-2 expansion strategy** as the priority growth lever.

---

### 📋 Plan Performance Analysis

| Plan Tier | Performance | Status |
|---|---|---|
| 🥇 P1 — Premium Plans | Consistently highest revenue | 🚀 Invest & Scale |
| 🥈 P2–P6 — Mid Tier | Stable performance | ✅ Maintain |
| ❌ P7–P9 — Legacy Plans | Sharp decline post 5G | 🗑️ Phase Out |

> **Insight:** Legacy plans are cannibalizing premium adoption. Phasing out P7–P9 and migrating users to premium plans could directly boost ARPU.

---

### 👥 Customer Adoption & Churn Behavior

```
5G Users    → ~23% LOWER churn rate vs 4G users
4G Users    → Higher churn, at risk of competitor switching
```

> **Insight:** 5G is a **retention tool** — customers on 5G are significantly more loyal. Accelerating 5G adoption among at-risk 4G customers should be a top priority.

---

### 🌐 Network KPIs → Revenue Connection

| KPI | Performance | Business Impact |
|---|---|---|
| ⚡ Speed | 500+ Mbps | Drives premium plan adoption |
| 📶 Latency | Low | Supports high-value enterprise clients |
| ⏱️ Uptime | High | Directly linked to customer retention |

> **Insight:** Strong network performance isn't just technical — it **directly supports revenue and retention.** Network investment ROI is measurable through premium adoption rates.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

| Priority | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| 🥇 1 | **Expand aggressively in Tier-2 cities** — Lucknow, Patna, Raipur showing clear post-5G growth |
| 🥈 2 | **Phase out legacy plans P7–P9** — they're declining and cannibalizing premium revenue |
| 🥉 3 | **Target 4G users for 5G migration** — 23% churn reduction justifies migration incentives |
| 4️⃣ 4 | **Develop metro-specific strategy** — Delhi & Chennai need competitive positioning, not just 5G rollout |
| 5️⃣ 5 | **Invest in P1 premium plan marketing** — highest ROI segment, consistent outperformer |
